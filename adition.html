Addition is putting together.


Example: 7 + 8

Let's learn the steps to solve this problem.

  1. Start with the number 7
  2. To add 8 to 7, you can count up from 7, 8 times.
  3. Counting up: 8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15
  4. So, 7 + 8 = 15
  5. Another way is by group adding
  6. Break 8 into 3 and 5
  7. First, add 3 to 7, 7 + 3 = 10
  8. Then add 5 to 10, 10 + 5 = 15
  9. Did you notice that both answers were the same?

Try these addition questions

1) 6 + 5 = ?

6 + 5 = 11 because if you count up 5 times from 6, you get 11.

2) 8 + 7 = ?

8 + 7 = 15 because if you break 7 into 2 and 5, then do 8 + 2, which is 10, then do 10 + 5, the answer is 15

3) 9 + 9 = ?

9 + 9 = 18 because if you count up 9 times from 9, you get 18

4) 7 + 8 = ?

7 + 8 = 15 because if you break 8 into 3 and 5, and do 7 + 3, which is 10, then do 10 + 5, you get 15
