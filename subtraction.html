Subtraction is taking away.


Example: 15-7

Let's learn the steps to solve this problem.

  1. Start with the number 15
  2. To take 7 away from 15, you can count back from 15 7 times.
  3. Counting back: 14, 13, 12, 11, 10, 9, 8
  4. So, 15 - 7 = 8
  5. Another way is by group subtracting
  6. Break 7 into 5 and 2
  7. First, subtract 5 from 15, 15 - 5 = 10
  8. Then subtract 2 from 10, 10 - 2 = 8
  9. Did you notice that both answers where the same?
